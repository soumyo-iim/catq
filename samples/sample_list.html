Question 1: Which planet is known as the Red Planet?

  • A) Earth
  • B) Mars (Correct)
  • C) Jupiter
  • D) Venus
Mars is known as the Red Planet due to the iron oxide (rust) on its surface, which gives it a reddish appearance.
