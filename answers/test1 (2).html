Arithmetic Test 2- Ratio & Proportion (LOD 1)

Question 1

An inheritance of Rs x was distributed among four charities, Charity A, Charity B, Charity C, and Charity D, in the ratio of 1/3 : 1/5 : 1/6 : 1/9. If the difference between the amounts received by Charity B and Charity D is Rs 832, then what is the value of x?

Options:

  • 1. Rs 7792
  • 2. Rs 7692
  • 3. Rs 7592
  • 4. Rs 7892

Question 2

If a sum of Rs 1,180 is to be divided among A, B and C, such that 2 times A’s share, 5 times B’s share and 7 times C’s share, are equal, then A’s share is

Options:

  • 1. Rs 500
  • 2. Rs 600
  • 3. Rs 700
  • 4. Rs 800

Question 3

In a pantry, there are packets of rice and packets of pasta. The total number of packets is 12. The number of rice packets to pasta packets is in the ratio of 1:2. If each rice packet costs Rs 10 and each pasta packet costs Rs 50, what is the total value of all the packets in the pantry?

Options:

  • 1. 110 Rs
  • 2. 220 Rs
  • 3. 330 Rs
  • 4. 440 Rs

Question 4

In the collection of a certain bookstore, the ratio of fiction books to non-fiction books was initially 3 to 8. After three fiction books were removed from the collection and two non-fiction books were added, the new ratio became 1 to 3. How many non-fiction books were there originally?

Options:

  • 1. 33
  • 2. 44
  • 3. 66
  • 4. 88

Question 5

If a : b is 2 : 5, b : c is 3 : 5 and c : d is 5 : 4, what is the ratio a : b : c : d?

Options:

  • 1. 6 : 15 : 25 : 10
  • 2. 12 : 15 : 25 : 20
  • 3. 6 : 15 : 25 : 20
  • 4. 6 : 15 : 25 : 40
