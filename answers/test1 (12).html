Question 2

A container initially holds 100 liters of pure fruit juice. First, 10 liters of water is added, and then 20 liters of the solution is removed. Next, 30 liters of water is added, and again 20 liters of the solution is removed. Find the amount of fruit juice (in liters) remaining in the solution now.

Options:

  • 1. 68.18 liters
  • 2. 65.18 liters
  • 3. 74.18 liters
  • 4. 60.18 liters

Question 3

A barrel initially contains 60 liters of pure honey. 12 liters of honey is removed and replaced with syrup. This process is repeated twice. Find the amount of honey (in liters) remaining in the barrel now.

Options:

  • 1. 30.4 liters
  • 2. 34.4 liters
  • 3. 38.4 liters
  • 4. 42.4 liters
