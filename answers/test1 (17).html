Question 2

Garima had only Rs. 200. Rs. 500 and Rs. 2000 notes in her wallet. She goes to Shoppers Stop. purchases some dresses and gives half of her Rs. 2000 notes & in turn receives the same number of Rs. 200 notes. She then goes to a restaurant and gives all her Rs. 500 notes and receives thirty Rs. 2000 notes, which increases the number of Rs. 2000 notes she had by 75%. Now if she has fifty Rs. 200 notes. What was the original number of Rs. 2000 notes she had at the start?

Options:

  • 1. 60
  • 2. 65
  • 3. 80
  • 4. 70

Question 3

Two friends, Riya and Tina, jointly own a bakery. The ratio of their ownership (In terms of area) in the bakery is 3:2. They produce two types of baked goods: cakes and cookies. The ratio of the area in the bakery dedicated to cakes and cookies for the entire bakery is 7:3. On Riya’s portion of the bakery, the ratio of cakes to cookies is 4:1. What is the ratio of cakes to cookies for Tina’s portion?

Options:

  • 1. 13:10
  • 2. 10:9
  • 3. 11:9
  • 4. 17:14

On Sunday, 1/5 ​ of the players who were absent on Saturday joined the practice session, while the rest remained absent. 1/5 ​of the players who were present on Saturday were absent, while the rest continued to attend. What fraction of the players attended the coaching session on Sunday?

Options:

  • 1. 13/25
